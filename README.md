# Auto-margin

Auto-margin is a platform for professional vehicle traders and dealerships in Europe.
It helps teams review incoming offers faster, compare vehicles against live market signals, and focus on deals with stronger margin potential.

## What This Repository Is

This repository contains the public-facing Auto-margin website and related product presentation layers, including:

- landing pages
- pricing and sales pages
- guidebook and documentation content
- contact and lead capture flows
- multilingual website content

It exists to represent the Auto-margin product online and communicate how the platform works, who it is for, and how users can learn more.

## Product Positioning

Auto-margin is built for:

- independent dealers
- trading companies
- import/export businesses
- dealer groups
- B2B automotive marketplaces

The platform is designed to reduce manual review, improve decision speed, and help buying teams evaluate vehicles with better market context.
