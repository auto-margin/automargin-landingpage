# AutoMargin

AutoMargin is a data-driven analytics platform designed for professional vehicle traders and dealerships operating across the European market. The platform helps dealers identify profitable car deals by analyzing real market prices, competitor listings, and potential resale margins before purchasing inventory.

AutoMargin aggregates live market data from major European automotive marketplaces and transforms it into clear, actionable insights. Instead of manually comparing listings across multiple platforms, dealers can instantly evaluate whether a vehicle represents a profitable opportunity.

The platform combines real-time market intelligence, AI-powered analysis, and pricing insights to support faster and more confident purchasing decisions.

---

# Mission

AutoMargin exists to remove uncertainty from the vehicle sourcing process.

Car dealers often spend hours manually researching listings, estimating resale value, and calculating potential profit. AutoMargin automates this process by providing accurate market comparisons and margin analysis in seconds.

The goal is simple:

Help dealers buy better cars at the right price.

---

# Core Product

AutoMargin provides a dashboard and analysis tools that allow users to evaluate car offers across multiple European markets.

Users can enter a vehicle description and instantly receive:

- Real market price comparisons
- Estimated resale value
- Potential profit margins
- Competitor listings
- Market demand indicators

This allows dealerships to make faster and more informed purchasing decisions.
